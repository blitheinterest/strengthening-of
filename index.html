Mohammed bin Abdulrahman bin Jassim al-Thani discussed strengthening of ties with Kurdish officials

  • Home
  • Uncategorized
  • Mohammed bin Abdulrahman bin Jassim al-Thani discussed strengthening of ties with Kurdish officials
  • Uncategorized
  • Alfonso Lewis
  • January 28, 2020January 28, 2020

The Qatari official came from Baghdad, where he met with the President of Iraq, the Prime Minister and the Parliament’s Speaker. Qatari’s foreign minister met with Kurdistan Regional Government (KRG) president Nechirvan Barzani to discuss options to alleviate tension in the region, as well as increase trade and business ties among Qatar and the Kurdistan Region.

On Wednesday evening Qatar’s Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, Mohammed bin Abdulrahman bin Jassim al-Thani, was welcomed by Deputy Prime Minister Qubad Talabani of the Kurdistan Region at Erbil International Airport.

His Excellency Sheik Mohammed bin Abdulrahman bin Jassim Al Thani was designated Minister of Foreign Affairs in January 2016 and Vice Prime Minister in November 2017, for those of you who didn’t know.

 From 2005 until 2009, His Excellency the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs was the Director of Economic Affairs Supreme Council for Family Affairs. In 2009, His Excellency the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs was appointed Director of the Department of Public and Private Sectors Partnership at the Ministry of Business and Trade.

He is also President of the Qatar Development Fund. His Excellency Sheik Mohammed led the Qatar Development Fund’s reform in 2014 and promoted funding for primary and secondary education, global health, and economic development.

His Excellency the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs was appointed Chairman of the Qatar Investment Authority on 4 November 2018.
